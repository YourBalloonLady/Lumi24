Frequently Asked Questions
Are your products for human consumption?

No. All products sold by Lumina are strictly supplied for laboratory research purposes only. They are not approved for human consumption, medical use, or personal use.

How do I pay?

Payment is currently via secure UK bank transfer only. Once you complete checkout, full payment instructions are sent directly to your email.

When will my order be dispatched?

Orders placed before 3PM are usually dispatched the same working day where possible. Orders placed after 3PM are usually dispatched the next working day.

Is shipping discreet?

Yes — all orders are shipped in plain, discreet packaging with no unnecessary reference to the contents on the outside.

What is included with my order?

Included items, if any, are listed on each product page. All products are supplied strictly for laboratory research use only and are not for human consumption.

Do you provide usage, dosing, or administration advice?

No. We do not provide guidance on usage, dosing, administration, protocols, or personal use. Our products are supplied strictly for laboratory research use only and are not for human consumption.

Can I track my delivery?

Yes — once your order has been dispatched, tracking information is provided where applicable.

Do you ship outside the UK?

Currently we only ship within the UK to keep fulfilment straightforward and reliable.
